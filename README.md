# ⚽ African Football Manager (AFM)

African Football Manager (AFM) is a modern football management simulation game focused on African football.

Unlike traditional football management games that concentrate primarily on Europe, AFM places African leagues, clubs, players, academies, and continental competitions at the center of the experience.

Players take control of a football club and manage every aspect of the organization, including transfers, tactics, youth development, finances, scouting, infrastructure, media relations, and matchday decisions.

---

## 🌍 Vision

To become the most comprehensive football management game dedicated to African football while providing a deep and immersive managerial experience comparable to leading football management titles.

AFM aims to celebrate African football by featuring:

- African leagues
- African clubs
- African players
- African academies
- African football culture
- Continental competitions

The project seeks to provide representation that is often limited in mainstream football management games.
